Track your Tim Hortons rim rolls and share your winnings with the world.

== Description ==

Brag about your epic winning streak or keep tabs on the great misfortune of receiving Please Play again time and again. Either way, bring this great Canadian seasonal game to the blogosphere.

== Installation ==

1. Unpack the plugin zip and upload the contents to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory. Alternatively, upload the plugin zip via the install screen within the WordPress Plugins manager
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins manager in WordPress
3. Add the Rim Diary widget to your blog and keep tabs on your rims from the Rim Diary page in the WP Admin.

== Screenshots ==

1. Click the buttons of the respective prizes to log your rims.
2. Show the world how you've fared in this great Canadian seasonal game.
